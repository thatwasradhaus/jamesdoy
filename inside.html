Inside Industry


Print | Editorial | Publishing

 

For almost a year I worked on laying out and artworking Inside Industry Magazine producing article layouts and adverts for a number of major companies including Mercedes Benz, BMW, Porsche, Austrian Mint and ABB. This involved liasing directly with the companies to produce something that they were happy to use to promote themselves. This was a test of many of my skills as it's sometimes pretty difficult to make cement or gas piping look interesting. I feel like I did as best I could!
